How FWD Works

Simple workflow, complete visibility

  1. Step 1

    Donor Posts Listing

    Add quantity, low-cost donation price, expiry, and exact pickup pin on map.

  2. Step 2

    NGO Finds & Claims

    Filter by location/category and check distance before claiming an order.

  3. Step 3

    Pickup & Close Loop

    Use built-in chat to coordinate and mark listing as collected/received.

Role Journeys

Designed for both Donors and NGOs

Donor Journey

  • 1. Add listing with quantity, price, address, and map pin.
  • 2. Respond to NGO messages for pickup timing.
  • 3. Track status transitions from available to collected.

NGO Journey

  • 1. Filter listings and verify map distance/ETA.
  • 2. Claim suitable order and coordinate through chat.
  • 3. Mark received and maintain claim history records.
